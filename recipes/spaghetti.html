Biscotti

Cookie

Biscotti, known also as cantucci, are Italian almond biscuits
that originated in the Tuscan city of Prato.
They are twice-baked, oblong-shaped, dry, crunchy, and may be dipped in a drink, traditionally Vin Santo.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • almonds
  • eggs
  • Flour

Steps in preparation

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet ingredients
  3. Finish making the dough
  4. Stir in nuts
  5. Divide the dough
  6. Fry the dough
